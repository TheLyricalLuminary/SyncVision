AThe scalar leads.

One large 74. The vector explains it — it doesn't compete for attention.

BBars are weighted.

Each axis bar's width = its weight. Scene gets a wide track, Signal gets a thin strip. The visual is the math.

CFixed vs actionable.

Scene is fixed after upload (—). Rights and Lyrics show an action chevron (↑) that opens the relevant intake.

DUnscored is empty.

Lyrics shows — as value with an empty track. The space itself communicates "score not yet computed."

ABrief stays anchored.

Scene context is sticky in the left rail so the director can re-read while scrolling tracks. Mobile drops to top of the stack.

BPer-card state lives in the border.

Approved cards get a green left accent + green pill; passed cards dim; the leader pulses with the brand gradient.

CAI reasoning as pull-quote.

Big serif quote glyph, italic Instrument Serif body, bolded turn. Treats AI like an opinion, not a debug log.

DDecision summary is live.

The brief panel's tally updates as the director clicks Approve / Pass — visible accountability without a confirmation modal.
